Smart Home Installation

Want your home to obey what you say and stay that way?

Consider it “obedience training” for your smart home.

Most “smart homes” are really just a pile of devices that don’t talk to each other. You end up with five different apps, automations that rarely work, and devices that go offline for no obvious reason. That’s not smart—that’s frustrating.

TNT Fix specializes in true home automation—where everything works together reliably. No app juggling, no “Can’t connect to device” messages, and no guesswork. Just a home that quietly does what you need while respecting your privacy.

Whether you're starting from scratch or trying to tame an existing setup, we’ll design, install, and configure a system that fits the way you actually live.
