What This Service Includes

Home Assistant is the most powerful way to unify your smart home—without relying on the cloud. We install it, connect your devices, and build automations that fit how you actually live.

  • Install Home Assistant on your hardware (or we can provide a dedicated device for an additional cost).
  • Connect and configure your existing smart devices and integrations.
  • Create a clean starter dashboard for phone/tablet (and wall panel if desired).
  • Set up automations and scenes (from basic schedules to advanced presence-based logic).
  • Voice assistant integration (Alexa / Google / Siri where supported).
  • Hands-on training so you can manage it confidently.

Pricing Tiers

  • Basic Setup: $599 (up to ~20 devices, basic automations, starter dashboard, voice integration, ~2 hours training)
  • Advanced Setup: $799 (up to ~50 devices, advanced automations, custom dashboards, energy monitoring options, ~4 hours training/support)
  • Complete Migration: $899 (migrate from other platforms, rebuild automations, optimize network, backups/recovery, advanced features like Node-RED if needed, ~6 hours training/support)

We’ll recommend the right tier after a quick audit of your devices and goals.

Good Fit For

  • Homes with mixed brands that don’t play nice together.
  • Anyone tired of devices going offline or automations breaking.
  • Privacy-focused homeowners who want local control (works even if the internet is down).
  • People who want dashboards, wall panels, and “real” automation—not just voice commands.

Not the Best Fit If…

  • You only want one or two devices controlled by Alexa/Google and don’t need deeper automation.
  • You’re looking for monitored alarm services (we can integrate devices, but we don’t sell monitoring).
