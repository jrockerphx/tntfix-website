Smart Lighting Setup

Install smart bulbs and switches, build scenes, and dial in schedules—so your lights just work (and save energy doing it).

Smart Home 2 – 3 hours Starting at $399 Serving Phoenix Metro, Coolidge & Casa Grande

What This Service Includes

Smart lighting is one of the easiest, most impactful upgrades you can make. We install and configure bulbs, switches, and hubs so everything is organized, reliable, and easy to control.

  • Install and configure up to 10 smart bulbs/switches (base service).
  • Set up lighting hubs like Philips Hue Bridge (or similar).
  • Configure apps on your phone/tablet.
  • Connect lighting to Alexa, Google Home, or Siri (where supported).
  • Create custom scenes (movie night, evening, away, etc.).
  • Program schedules and automations.
  • Group lights into rooms/zones for simple control.
  • Testing + training before we leave.

Why Choose Smart Lighting?

  • Energy savings: smart LED bulbs use far less energy than incandescents and can be scheduled so lights aren’t left on.
  • Security: automatic “away” lighting patterns help deter intruders.
  • Ambiance: set the perfect mood with dimming and color options.
  • Convenience: control lights from your phone—even when you’re not home.

Pricing

  • Additional bulbs/switches: $15 per device.

  • Philips Hue: premium ecosystem and reliability.
  • LIFX: bright, vibrant colors; often no hub required.

  • TP-Link Kasa: excellent switches and dependable app experience.
