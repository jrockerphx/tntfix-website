Not Sure Where to Start? Let's Talk.

Home repairs can be overwhelming—especially when you're not sure what's involved, what it'll cost, or whether you even need a professional. That's why we offer a free 15-minute consultation. No sales pitch, no pressure. Just honest answers to help you figure out your next step.

What We Can Cover in 15 Minutes

  • Project feasibility: Can this be done? Is it a DIY job or do you need help?
  • Rough estimates: Ballpark pricing so you know what to budget
  • Scope clarification: Break down what's actually involved in your project
  • Priority guidance: Which repairs are urgent vs. can wait
  • Materials advice: What to buy (and what to avoid)
  • Handyman vs. licensed contractor: Help you understand when each is appropriate
  • Timeline expectations: How long jobs typically take
  • Smart home questions: Is automation right for your situation?

How It Works

  • Book your slot: Pick a time that works for you
  • Phone or video call: Your choice—whatever's easiest
  • Describe your situation: Tell us what's going on
  • Get honest feedback: We'll share what we know
  • No obligation: Book a service, DIY it, or call someone else—totally up to you

Great Questions to Ask

Make the most of your 15 minutes with questions like:

  • "I have a list of small repairs—can you help me prioritize?"
  • "Is this something I can fix myself, or should I hire someone?"
  • "What would it cost to install [specific item]?"
  • "Do I need a licensed contractor for this, or can a handyman handle it?"
  • "How long would [project] typically take?"
  • "What should I look for when buying [materials/fixtures]?"
  • "Is my honey-do list realistic for a half-day or full-day booking?"
  • "Can you help with smart home stuff, or is that separate?"

What This Isn't

To keep consultations helpful and focused:

  • Not an on-site inspection (that's a separate service if needed)
  • Not a detailed written quote (we can provide those after discussing scope)
  • Not unlimited time—we'll stick close to 15 minutes out of respect for everyone's schedule
  • Not a commitment—you're free to take our advice and run with it however you'd like

Why We Offer This

Honestly? Because it helps everyone. You get answers without risking money on the wrong solution. We get to understand your needs before showing up. And when we do work together, we're both on the same page from the start. It's just a better way to do business.
