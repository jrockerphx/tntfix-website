Smart Thermostat Installation & Setup

Control your comfort from anywhere and save on cooling costs with a professionally installed smart thermostat.

Smart Home Approx. 2 hours Starting at $349 Serving Phoenix Metro, Coolidge & Casa Grande

What This Service Includes

Arizona’s brutal heat demands more than a basic thermostat. A smart thermostat can save an estimated 10–23% on cooling costs while keeping your home more comfortable and consistent. We handle the entire setup so you don’t have to worry about wiring, apps, or configuration.

  • Professional removal of your old thermostat and installation of your new smart thermostat.
  • Proper low-voltage wiring verification and correction where needed.
  • Wi-Fi network connection and app setup on your phone or tablet.
  • Basic scheduling setup tailored to your daily routine.
  • Voice assistant integration with Alexa, Google, or Siri (if desired).
  • Home Assistant integration where applicable for advanced automation.
  • Full walkthrough and hands-on training so you know how to use your new thermostat confidently.

What to Expect During Your Visit

We treat your home and your time with respect. You’ll know exactly what’s happening at each step.

  • Arrival within the agreed service window and a quick review of your comfort goals.
  • Inspection of your existing thermostat wiring and HVAC equipment for compatibility.
  • Clean, careful installation of the new thermostat and any required trim plate or adapter.
  • Step-by-step setup of apps, Wi-Fi, accounts, and schedules while you follow along.
  • Testing for proper cooling and heating operation after installation.
  • Time reserved at the end for questions, adjustments, and tips to maximize savings.

Good Fit For

  • Homeowners who want lower cooling bills without sacrificing comfort.
  • Anyone who wants to control their thermostat from a phone, tablet, or voice assistant.
  • Homes already using smart devices that want to add smart climate control.
  • People who are comfortable buying the thermostat but prefer a pro to install and configure it.

Not the Best Fit If…

  • You need a full HVAC system replacement rather than thermostat control improvements.
  • Your system is extremely old or non-standard and not compatible with smart thermostats.
  • You require work that must be performed by a licensed HVAC contractor beyond thermostat wiring.

Popular Smart Thermostat Brands We Work With

  • Ecobee – Our favorite for overall reliability, features, and room sensors.
  • Google Nest – Great if you’re already invested in the Google Home ecosystem.
  • Honeywell T9 – A strong budget-friendly option with remote sensors.
  • Emerson Sensi – Solid performance and app control at a reasonable price.

Need a C-Wire?

Many older Arizona homes don’t have the required C-wire for constant power, which is the #1 reason smart thermostat installs fail. If we discover you need a C-wire, we can schedule a separate C-Wire Installation service to get you powered up the right way.
