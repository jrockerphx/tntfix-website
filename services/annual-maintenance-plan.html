Why Maintenance Plans Make Sense in Phoenix

Phoenix A/C systems run harder and longer than almost anywhere in the country. Regular maintenance isn't optional—it's essential for preventing expensive mid-summer breakdowns.

  • Avoid emergency failures: catch problems during scheduled visits, not at 110°F
  • Lower energy bills: maintained systems use 10-25% less energy
  • Extend equipment life: regular care adds 3-5 years to system lifespan
  • Maintain warranty coverage: many warranties require annual professional maintenance
  • Better indoor air quality: regular filter changes and cleaning included

What Each Tune-Up Includes

Every maintenance visit includes our comprehensive 22-point inspection:

  • Electrical components and connections
  • Refrigerant pressure and temperature
  • Capacitors, contactors, and relays
  • Thermostat calibration and operation
  • Condensate drain inspection and treatment
  • Air filter replacement
  • Safety controls and switches
  • Blower motor and fan operation
  • Full system performance test
  • Written report with recommendations
