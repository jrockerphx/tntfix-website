Basic Electrical

Modernize switches, outlets, and lighting with clean, tested installs.

Home Repair 1 – 3 hours Project-based Serving Phoenix Metro, Coolidge & Casa Grande

What This Service Includes

  • Replacement of worn or outdated switches and outlets
  • Installation of dimmer switches and modern switch covers
  • Removal and installation of light fixtures (customer-provided)
  • Ceiling fan light kit additions or replacements
  • Installation of smart switches and dimmers (we'll configure basics)
  • GFCI outlet installation in kitchens and bathrooms
  • Testing with voltage meter to ensure safe operation
  • Verification that circuits are properly grounded
  • Basic troubleshooting of non-working outlets or switches

What’s Not Included

  • Service panel upgrades or breaker replacements
  • Running new circuits from the panel
  • Whole-house rewiring or aluminum wire remediation
  • Outdoor electrical work requiring permits
  • Installation of EV chargers or high-voltage appliances
  • Generator installation or transfer switches
  • Work on main service lines from the utility
  • Electrical work requiring licensed electrician sign-off

Best For

  • Homeowners wanting to modernize old switches/outlets
  • Anyone upgrading to smart home compatible switches
  • People with outlets that don't grip plugs anymore
  • Households replacing dated light fixtures with modern ones
  • Anyone wanting dimmer control in living areas
  • DIYers who got stuck halfway through an upgrade

Not the Best Fit If…

  • Anyone needing permitted electrical work for new construction
  • Projects requiring panel upgrades or new circuits
  • Situations requiring licensed electrician certification
  • Complex three-way switch configurations in multiple locations
  • Work on houses with known aluminum wiring issues

What to Expect During the Visit

We shut off power at the breaker, verify it’s off with a voltage tester, remove old devices, and install your new ones. We test everything before closing up. Most switch/outlet swaps take 15–30 minutes each. Light fixtures vary based on size and complexity, and we’ll walk you through any wiring quirks before proceeding.

How to Prepare

  • Have new switches, outlets, or fixtures unboxed and ready
  • Know which breakers control which rooms (or we'll figure it out)
  • Clear the area below ceiling work (we might need a ladder)
  • Make a list of all the switches/outlets you want updated
  • Tell us about any outlets or switches that don't work right
  • Have a flashlight handy in case we need to kill power to a room
