What Are Mini-Splits?

Mini-split systems (also called ductless heat pumps) consist of an outdoor compressor unit connected to one or more indoor air handlers. They provide efficient heating and cooling without ductwork, making them ideal for:

  • Room additions and sunrooms
  • Garages and workshops
  • Casitas and guest houses
  • Homes without existing ductwork
  • Zone-by-zone temperature control
  • Replacing inefficient window units

Why Choose Mini-Splits?

  • Energy efficiency: inverter technology adjusts compressor speed for optimal efficiency
  • Zone control: heat or cool specific rooms independently
  • No ductwork needed: perfect for additions or homes without ducts
  • Quiet operation: whisper-quiet indoor units
  • Year-round comfort: heating and cooling in one system
  • Smart features: WiFi control, schedules, and integration available
  • Clean installation: just a 3" line set hole, no major construction

Phoenix-Specific Considerations

Mini-splits work exceptionally well in Phoenix's climate, but proper sizing and installation are critical:

  • Adequate BTU capacity: Phoenix heat requires properly sized units
  • SEER ratings: high-efficiency models pay for themselves quickly
  • Outdoor unit shade: we help select cooler locations to maximize efficiency
  • Line set protection: UV-resistant insulation for exposed runs
  • Backup cooling: discuss integration with existing systems if desired
