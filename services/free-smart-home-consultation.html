Curious About Smart Home? Let's Figure It Out Together.

Smart home technology can be exciting—and confusing. There are dozens of platforms, hundreds of devices, and endless YouTube videos telling you different things. Before you buy a bunch of gadgets that don't work together, let's have a quick conversation. We'll help you cut through the noise and find what actually makes sense for your home and lifestyle.

What We Can Cover in 15 Minutes

  • Where to start: Best first steps based on your goals and budget
  • Platform guidance: Alexa vs. Google vs. Apple vs. local control—what's right for you?
  • Device recommendations: Which brands and products are worth the money
  • Compatibility check: Will this work with what you already have?
  • Wi-Fi reality check: Is your network ready for smart devices?
  • Automation ideas: What's possible and what's actually useful day-to-day
  • DIY vs. professional: What you can set up yourself vs. where help makes sense
  • Budget planning: How to phase in smart home upgrades over time
  • Privacy & security: Honest talk about cloud vs. local, data concerns, and keeping things secure

How It Works

  • Book your slot: Pick a time that works for you
  • Phone or video call: Whatever's easier—video helps if you want to show us your current setup
  • Tell us your goals: What problems are you trying to solve? What sounds cool?
  • Get honest recommendations: We'll share what we'd actually do in your situation
  • No obligation: Take our advice and DIY, book with us, or do nothing—totally your call

Great Questions to Ask

Make the most of your 15 minutes with questions like:

  • "I want to start with smart lighting—what should I buy?"
  • "Will [specific device] work with my [existing system]?"
  • "What's the difference between Zigbee, Z-Wave, and Wi-Fi devices?"
  • "Is a smart thermostat worth it with my HVAC system?"
  • "Can I control everything from one app?"
  • "How do I make my garage door smart?"
  • "What's the best video doorbell that doesn't require a subscription?"
  • "I'm renting—what smart home stuff can I take with me when I move?"
  • "How do I automate my lights to turn on at sunset?"
  • "Is Home Assistant worth learning, or should I stick with Alexa/Google?"
