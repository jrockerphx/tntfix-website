What Filter Do I Need?

Not sure what size or type of filter you need? No problem. When you sign up, we'll help you determine:

  • Exact dimensions: filter sizes like 16x25x1, 20x20x1, 14x30x1, etc.
  • MERV rating: typically MERV 8, 11, or 13 based on your system and needs
  • Filter type: pleated, electrostatic, or specialty filters
  • Quantity: how many filters your system requires

If you've had a tune-up or filter service from us previously, we already have your filter specs on file and can set up your subscription in minutes.

Phoenix Filter Replacement Guidelines

Phoenix's desert environment is tough on HVAC filters. Here's what we typically recommend:

  • Standard homes: 30-60 day replacement schedule
  • Homes with pets: 30 day replacement (pet hair clogs filters faster)
  • Allergy/asthma concerns: 30 day replacement with MERV 11-13 filters
  • During monsoon season: check filters more frequently (dust storms accelerate clogging)
  • New construction nearby: 30 day replacement (construction dust is brutal on filters)
  • Vacation homes: 90 day replacement (minimal use)
