Remove dirt, debris, and buildup that robs your A/C of efficiency and cooling power.

What This Service Includes

Dirty coils are one of the leading causes of poor cooling performance and high energy bills in Phoenix. We thoroughly clean both outdoor condenser coils and indoor evaporator coils to restore efficiency.

  • Visual inspection of outdoor condenser unit
  • Remove debris, leaves, and grass clippings from unit
  • Professional coil cleaning solution application
  • High-pressure rinse of condenser coils
  • Straighten bent coil fins where accessible
  • Clean evaporator coil (indoor unit)
  • Check condensate drain for blockages
  • Verify proper airflow after cleaning
  • Test system performance improvement

Why Coil Cleaning Matters

In Phoenix's dusty, desert environment, A/C coils get dirty fast. Dirt buildup acts like insulation, preventing proper heat transfer and forcing your system to work harder. The results include:

  • Reduced cooling capacity: dirty coils can reduce cooling by 20-30%
  • Higher energy bills: system works longer to achieve desired temperature
  • Shorter equipment life: extra strain wears out components faster
  • Frozen evaporator coils: restricted airflow causes ice buildup
  • Compressor failure: overheating from poor heat transfer can kill the compressor

When Coils Need Cleaning

Most Phoenix homes need coil cleaning annually, some twice per year if you have:

  • Landscaping close to the outdoor unit
  • Trees that shed leaves or cotton
  • Pets that shed fur
  • High dust exposure from construction or desert winds
  • Indoor smokers or excessive cooking

Our Cleaning Process

We use professional-grade coil cleaners that break down dirt and organic buildup without damaging delicate aluminum fins. After chemical application, we carefully rinse from inside-out to push debris away from the coil rather than deeper into it. For the indoor coil, we access through the air handler and ensure proper drainage afterward.
