What This Service Includes

Arizona homes often have hot spots and cold spots due to sun exposure and layout. Smart climate zones give you room-by-room temperature control—without installing multiple AC units.

We install smart vents and temperature sensors in key rooms, then integrate them with your smart thermostat so airflow automatically shifts to where comfort is needed most.

  • Consultation and room analysis to identify problem areas.
  • Installation of 4–6 smart vents (Flair, Keen, or similar) where appropriate.
  • 4–6 room temperature sensors in key rooms.
  • Integration with your smart thermostat.
  • Automated zone scheduling tuned to your daily routine.
  • Optional Home Assistant integration for advanced control and dashboards.
  • Energy usage monitoring options and post-setup optimization.
  • Training so you know how to adjust zones and schedules.

Benefits

  • Stop wasting AC on empty rooms.
  • Eliminate hot spots in sun-facing rooms.
  • Reduce energy bills by roughly 15–30% (home-dependent).
  • Extend HVAC system lifespan by reducing overcooling and unnecessary runtime.

Example: Close bedroom vents during the day to boost cooling in living areas, then reverse at night for sleeping comfort.
