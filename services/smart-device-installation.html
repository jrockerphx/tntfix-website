Smart Device Installation

Get professional installation for your smart locks, cameras, switches, sensors, and more—so everything works together, reliably.

Smart Home Approx. 30 minutes – 2.5 hours Starting at $89 Serving Phoenix Metro, Coolidge & Casa Grande

What This Service Includes

You bring the smart devices; we make them actually smart. Instead of juggling multiple apps, confusing instructions, or half-working automations, we’ll install, configure, and integrate your devices into a cohesive system.

  • Unboxing, physical installation, and mounting of supported smart devices.
  • Wi-Fi network connection and verification of signal quality where devices are installed.
  • App setup on your phone or tablet, including account creation if needed.
  • Voice control integration with Alexa, Google, or Siri (where supported).
  • Basic automation setup (schedules, simple triggers, notifications).
  • Testing every device to ensure reliable operation before we leave.
  • Hands-on training so you understand how to use and manage your devices.

Devices We Commonly Install

  • Smart locks (August, Yale, Schlage, Kwikset).
  • Video doorbells (Ring, Nest, Arlo, and similar).
  • Indoor and outdoor security cameras.
  • Smart switches, dimmers, and smart plugs/outlets.
  • Motion sensors and contact sensors for doors and windows.
  • Garage door controllers and water leak detectors.
  • Smart smoke/CO detectors (where allowed by local regulations).

What to Expect During Your Visit

  • Review of your device list, goals, and preferred apps/voice assistants.
  • Physical installation, including drilling or minor mounting work as needed.
  • Network and app setup, plus device naming that makes sense in everyday use.
  • Basic automations (like “arrive home,” schedules, or simple motion triggers).
  • Final walkthrough showing how to use everything and how to add simple scenes later.

Good Fit For

  • Homeowners who already purchased smart devices but don’t want the hassle of installation.
  • People who want devices from multiple brands to work together smoothly.
  • Anyone who tried DIY setup and ended up frustrated, stuck, or half-configured.

Not the Best Fit If…

  • You require a fully engineered, enterprise-grade security system (we focus on residential smart devices).
  • You need complex low-voltage wiring runs beyond typical smart device installs.
  • You’re looking for monitored alarm services—we can often integrate devices, but we don’t sell monitoring.
