Small Plumbing Repairs

Fix drips, leaks, and under-sink annoyances before they turn into bigger problems.

Home Repair Varies Estimate at visit Serving Phoenix Metro, Coolidge & Casa Grande

What This Service Includes

  • Diagnosis and repair of dripping faucets (kitchen, bathroom, outdoor)
  • Replacement of worn toilet flappers, fill valves, and flush mechanisms
  • Repair or replacement of leaky supply lines under sinks
  • Garbage disposal removal and replacement (customer-provided unit)
  • Tightening or replacing drain assemblies and P-traps
  • Minor leak identification and repair under sinks
  • Testing all repairs for proper operation before completion
  • Basic cleanup of work area

What’s Not Included

  • Replacement of fixtures you haven't purchased (we can advise on what to buy)
  • Major pipe replacement or rerouting requiring wall/floor access
  • Main water line or sewer line work
  • Installation of new plumbing where none exists
  • Water heater repairs or replacement
  • Well pump or pressure tank work
  • Whole-house re-piping projects
  • Septic system work

Best For

  • Homeowners tired of that constant drip keeping them awake
  • Anyone who's been putting off a wobbly toilet handle for months
  • People who want to stop wasting water on leaky faucets
  • Households preparing for guests or getting ready to sell
  • DIYers who got halfway through and need rescue
  • Anyone who doesn't want to deal with the mess under the sink

Not the Best Fit If…

  • Emergency burst pipe situations requiring immediate response
  • Complex installations requiring permits or licensed plumbers
  • Anyone needing work on gas lines or tankless water heaters
  • Projects requiring excavation or concrete cutting
  • Insurance claims requiring licensed contractor documentation

What to Expect During the Visit

We arrive with common supplies and replacement parts. After a quick assessment, we explain what’s needed and provide an estimate before starting. We shut off water to the affected area, complete the repair, test thoroughly, and clean up. Most small repairs take about 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on what we find.

How to Prepare

  • Clear the area under sinks so we can access pipes easily
  • Have towels ready in case of minor water spillage
  • Know where your main water shut-off is located (we'll ask)
  • If you're replacing a fixture, have the new one unboxed and ready
  • Take photos of ongoing leaks to show the frequency/severity
  • Make a list of any other drips or plumbing annoyances while we're there
