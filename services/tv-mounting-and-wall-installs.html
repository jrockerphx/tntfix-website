Mount Types Explained

  • Fixed/Low-profile: TV sits flat against wall, minimal gap, cleanest look. Best for eye-level viewing where you won't need to adjust.
  • Tilting: Allows vertical angle adjustment. Great for mounting above eye level or reducing glare.
  • Full-motion/Articulating: Extends from wall, swivels left/right, tilts up/down. Best for corner mounts, multi-angle viewing, or accessing cables behind TV.
  • Ceiling mount: Suspended from ceiling. Used for commercial, outdoor patios, or unique room layouts. (Priced separatelyâ€”ask for quote)
